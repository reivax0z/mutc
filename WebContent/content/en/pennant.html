Pennant Competitions

Format Information

Standard: Advanced
Format: Singles & Doubles alternating weekly

  • Singles Week - One best-of-3 set match
  • Doubles Week - Two best-of-3 set matches
  • 14 Rounds + finals.
  • 7 rounds played at home, 7 rounds played away, generally alternating weekly

Season Details

Season: April to August
Deadline: Teams are formed and submitted in early March.
