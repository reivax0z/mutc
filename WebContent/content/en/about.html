Melbourne University Tennis Club

- About -

Established in the 1880's, we are one of the largest and most active sporting clubs at the University of Melbourne. Through our affiliation with Tennis Victoria, we aim to provide year-round tennis through internal and regional competitions, coaching, squad sessions, and court access. A number of social events are also arranged throughout the year for members and friends to enjoy, which has included Melbourne Park nights, Dinners, Trivia Nights, and the much-anticipated annual MUTC Cocktail Party.

Tennis Courts Location

MUTC's on-court activities (such as internal competitions, home matches for regional competitions, training and coaching) are held at the University of Melbourne (Parkville Campus) Tennis Courts.
