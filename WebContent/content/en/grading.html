Melbourne University Tennis Club

- Grading Guidelines -

The MUTC currently grades ALL new members into three broad categories – Advanced, Intermediate and Beginner. Before being eligible to enter a competition, any member that has not previously played in one of the competitions that the MUTC offers, must attend a grading session.

Grading sessions will be held just prior to, and at the beginning of, each Melbourne University Semester. Please e-mail melbunitennis@gmail.com to find out the dates for upcoming grading sessions.

Grading Guidelines

ADVANCED

Profile:

  • Can sustain a fast paced rally & hit powerfully and consistently.
  • Has a strong first serve and a consistent second serve.

Eligible for:

  • Tennis Victoria Pennant (External)
  • BDNTA (External) – Higher grades only
  • BRTA (External)
  • Tuesday Night Doubles (Internal)
  • Friday Advanced Singles (Internal)

INTERMEDIATE

Profile:

  • Can sustain a slow paced rally & hit consistently.
  • Has a consistent serve.

Eligible for:

  • BDNTA (External) – Lower grades only
  • Thursday Night Doubles (Internal)
  • Friday Intermediate Singles (Internal)

BEGINNER

Does not meet an INTERMEDIATE standard.

Eligible for:

  • Coaching with HSTA
  • Beginners Hitting Sessions
