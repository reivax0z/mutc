Eligible Non-Students

The following groups fall into the category of Eligible Non-Students:

  • University of Melbourne Graduates
  • Graduate of other Universities
  • Current Students of other Universities
  • University Staff members
  • Staff not on University payroll but employed on site(s) by an organisation which has a permanent office on the University sites
  • Resident, member or staff of the University's affiliated colleges and halls of residence
  • Leave of absence and/or deferred students of the University of Melbourne (not exceeding 4 years from the date on which they
    • cease to be an enrolled student)
  • Immediate family of current students or eligible non students - siblings, parents, partners, children
  • MUSA Honorary Life Member
  • Eligible under Director's discretion (for those whose involvement would greatly benefit the club)

Membership Benefits

Opportunities to participate in an extensive range of on-court activities:

  • Internal competitions for all levels from beginner to advanced
  • Regional and state level competitions
  • Local, state and national tournaments
  • Access to squad and competition training
  • Access to professional grade ball machine for training
  • Discounted coaching

Opportunities to meet your perfect match...

  • Regular social events including pub nights and BBQ lunches
  • Annual Infamous Cocktail Party
  • End-Of-Season Celebrations for competition players
  • And many more events...

Other Membership benefits:

  • Discounted restringing available to club members
  • Discounted gym membership for competition players
  • 10% discount at Prelli Racquets
  • Access to Australian Open Tickets before they go on sale to the public
  • Access to the Davis Cup preferential ticketing (when held in Victoria)
  • Access to the Wimbledon Ballot
  • Tennis Ticketing Offers
  • Monthly Prize Draw
  • Discounts and Special Offers
