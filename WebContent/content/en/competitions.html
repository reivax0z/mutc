Melbourne University Tennis Club

- Competitions -

Melbourne University Tennis Club offers a range of competition opportunities throughout the year, catering for a range of standards from intermediate to advanced players. With a competition or training sessions held on every day of the week, there will be a competition opportunity that suits you!



To participate in one of our competitions, please read the and come to one of our grading sessions at the beginning of the semester.

External Competitions

External competitions are competitions where the MUTC competes against other clubs around Melbourne. We currently field teams in three different competitions:

Tennis Victoria Pennant

Standard: Advanced
Format: Singles & Doubles alternating weekly

BDNTA - Blackburn & District Night Tennis Association - Monday Night Doubles

Standard: Competitive to Advanced
Format: Singles & Doubles

BRTA - Bayside Regional Tennis Association - Saturday Summer Competition

Standard: Competitive to Advanced
Format: Singles & Doubles

Internal Competitions

Open Advanced Doubles

Tuesday: 6-8pm and 8-10pm
This is for the advanced tennis players with competitive experience.
The competition provides a relaxing atmosphere so it's a great opportunity to meet new people as well as play some high quality tennis.

Open Intermediate Doubles

Thursday: 6-8pm and 8-10pm
For those who can sustain rallies but are relatively new to competition.
A fun competition and a great way to meet new people. You can start here and always look to move to the advanced doubles competition.

Open Advanced Singles

Friday: 5-7pm
This is for advanced tennis players with competitive experience only.
The competition is limited to 12 places and priority will be given to those who enter and pay first.

Open Intermediate Singles

Friday: 7-9pm
This is for intermediate / competitive tennis players.
The competition is limited to 12 places and priority will be given to those who enter and pay first.

MUTC's Beginner Hitting Sessions

A new initiative run on an ad-hoc basis, MUTC's Beginner Hitting Sessions ('BHS') are run by the dedicated and friendly MUTC committee.

BHS consists of a fortnightly casual hit with one or more of MUTC's committee members. We bring out 'SAM', our resident ball machine, as well as some funky music to create a fun and friendly atmosphere for those new to the sport of tennis.

Cost: $10 for non-members, $5 for MUTC members.
